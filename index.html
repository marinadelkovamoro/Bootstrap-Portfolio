About Me

Born and raised in Bulgaria, I could never imagine life would take me to the U.S. at the age of 20. I got my high-school diploma in Plovdiv and then transferred to Sofia University, Bulgaria. Two years later I arrived to San Diego for a work-and-travel summer adventure. I moved to the States for good, re-started my academic career at SDSU and graduated as a Bachelor of Accountancy in 2016. In the meantime, I worked as an accountant and met my husband.

I am fluent in three languages: Bulgarian, English, and Russian, and understand approximately 80% of Serbo-Croatian and Greek. One of my goals is to become fluent in twose two languages by the end of 2020.

Another goal of mine is to get a job as a web developer after I finish the Coding Boot Camp at UCSD Extension.

My hobbies are: reading books, playing tennis, doing yoga, gardening, and listening to music.

Fun fact: I still cannot pronounce "literally" right.
